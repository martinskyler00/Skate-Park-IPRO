Current Job Openings

At GoGo's Skate Park, we're always on the lookout for dedicated and enthusiastic people to join our team. Check out our current job openings below and click on any position to learn more.

Event Coordinator

Plan, coordinate, and execute events at our skate park, ensuring every visitor has an amazing experience.

Skate Instructor

Share your passion for skateboarding by teaching skills and techniques to visitors of all ages and levels.

Marketing Specialist

Help us spread the word about GoGo's Skate Park by managing our marketing and social media campaigns.

Maintenance Technician

Keep our facilities safe and in top shape to ensure a smooth experience for all park-goers.

How to Apply

If you’re interested in any of the positions listed, please send your resume and a cover letter to careers@gogoskatepark.com. Be sure to mention the job title in your email subject line.

Don’t see the perfect position for you? We’re always interested in meeting people who are passionate about what we do. Feel free to send us your resume and let us know how you think you could make an impact!
