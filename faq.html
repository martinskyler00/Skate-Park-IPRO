Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Common Questions

1. What are the opening hours of GoGo's Skate Park?

We are open daily from 9:00 AM to 8:00 PM. Special events may extend hours on weekends.

2. Do I need to bring my own equipment?

We recommend bringing your own skateboard and protective gear. However, we do have rental options for skateboards and helmets available on-site.

3. Are there any age restrictions?

No, GoGo's Skate Park is open to all ages. We have designated areas for different age groups to ensure everyone can enjoy the park safely.

4. Do I need to sign a waiver to participate?

Yes, all participants must sign a waiver. If you're under 18, a parent or guardian will need to sign it for you. You can sign the waiver online to save time.

5. Can I host a private event or party at the park?

Absolutely! We offer private event packages for birthday parties, corporate events, and more. Please contact us for more details on booking.

6. Do you offer skating lessons?

Yes, we offer both group and private lessons for all skill levels. Check our event schedule for upcoming classes.
